Recognising the signs


Signs of radicalisation can vary, but common indicators include sudden changes in behaviour or appearance, becoming withdrawn from family and friends, expressing extreme views or intolerance, increased secrecy about activities or online behaviour, and associating with new groups or individuals who promote extremist ideas.
